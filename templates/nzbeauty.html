Who are We?

Lanreé Aesthetics offers more than just beauty appearance service with the emphasis of individualised personal care treatment. We offer an array of aesthetic dermatological procedures using latest cutting edges techniques in the industry.

Lanree Aesthetics is located at 128, Parnell Road, Parnell, one of Auckland’s most affluent suburbs in New Zealand, where timeless heritage rub shoulders with the bright, modern lights of tomorrow.
